Rahul Vishwakarma 

LinkedIn | CV | GitHub

Rahul Vishwakarma is a Software Engineer at Dell Technologies (formerly EMC2), where he drives solutions for Data Protection and helps customers protect their data with Data Domain (de-duplication based backup storage), and prior to this, he designed HPE ConvergedSystem for SAP HANA reference architecture at Hewlett Packard Enterprise. He loves solving challenging problems across interdisciplinary domains.

He holds a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from SRM Univerisity, India (2009) and had an offer of Doctor of Philosophy in Bioengineering from University of Canterbury, New Zealand (2012). He is one of the founding members of SNIA Blockchain Storage TWG and an active contributor at Smart Data Accelerator Interface (SDXI) TWG.
